## Purpose

Weekly synchronization for the ART to assess progress, manage dependencies, and address impediments.

---

## Pre-Meeting Preparation

1. Gather Feature status updates from each team
2. Collect impediment reports
3. Review ROAM board for updates
4. Check PI Objective progress

---

## Meeting Agenda (30-60 minutes)

### 1. PI Objective Progress (5 min)

- Quick status on committed objectives
- Highlight any at-risk objectives

### 2. Feature Status Review (10 min)

- Each team shares Feature status
- Focus on completed and blocked items

### 3. Cross-Team Dependencies (10 min)

- Review dependency board
- Confirm delivery commitments
- Identify new dependencies

### 4. Impediments & Blockers (10 min)

- Teams raise impediments
- Assign owners for resolution
- Escalate as needed

### 5. ROAM Risk Updates (5 min)

- Review active risks
- Update ROAM categories
- Add new risks

### 6. Action Items (5 min)

- Summarize action items
- Assign owners and due dates

---

## Outputs

Generate:

1. ART Sync notes with status updates
2. Updated dependency board
3. Action items list
4. Updated ROAM risk board

---

## Facilitation Tips

- Keep discussions brief - details offline
- Focus on blockers, not status reports
- Ensure cross-team visibility
- Track action items to completion
