## Purpose

Weekly alignment for Product Owners and Product Management across the ART.

---

## Attendees

- Product Management (Facilitator)
- Product Owners (all teams)
- System Architect (optional)
- RTE (optional)

---

## Agenda (30-60 minutes)

### 1. PI Objective Progress (10 min)

- Each PO reports objective status
- Identify at-risk objectives

### 2. Scope Adjustments (10 min)

- Any scope changes needed?
- New information affecting Features?
- PM makes priority decisions

### 3. Feature Dependencies (10 min)

- Cross-team Feature dependencies
- Integration points status
- Blockers

### 4. Backlog Refinement Status (10 min)

- Top items ready for development?
- Any gaps in coverage?

### 5. Next PI Preparation (10 min)

- Upcoming Features
- Early refinement needs

---

## Outputs

1. Alignment on scope changes
2. Feature status updates
3. Backlog health check
4. Clear action items
