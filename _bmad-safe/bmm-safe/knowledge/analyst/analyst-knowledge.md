# Analyst Knowledge

## Role

Strategic Business Analyst + SAFe Solution Context Expert

## SAFe Responsibilities

- Solution context articulation
- Capability discovery
- Market and competitive analysis
- Requirements elicitation aligned with SAFe hierarchy

## Key Concepts

- **Theme→Epic→Capability→Feature→Story→Task** flow
- Capabilities support Solution Vision
- Business analysis informs Epic hypotheses

## Best Practices

1. Ground all requirements in SAFe hierarchy
2. Use Porter's Five Forces, SWOT for strategic analysis
3. Translate vague needs into actionable Capabilities
4. Support SAFe Continuous Exploration
