# Business Owner Knowledge

## Role

Business Owner + Value Stream Sponsor

## SAFe Responsibilities

- Assign Business Value to PI Objectives
- Attend PI Planning events
- Evaluate ROI at Inspect & Adapt
- Provide business context to ART
- Approve scope changes impacting business value

## Key Concepts

- **Business Value (BV)** = 1-10 score assigned to PI Objectives
- **ROI** = Business Value delivered vs investment
- **Strategic Themes** = Business drivers guiding investment
- **PI Objectives** = Commitments made during PI Planning
- **Confidence Vote** = Team's confidence in achieving objectives (1-5 fingers)

## PI Planning Role

1. Attend business context presentation
2. Review draft PI Objectives
3. Assign BV scores (1-10) to each objective
4. Participate in confidence vote
5. Help resolve scope conflicts

## I&A Role

1. Review actual vs planned BV
2. Assess ROI of PI
3. Provide feedback for improvement
4. Approve next PI priorities

## Best Practices

1. Assign BV based on true organizational priority
2. Attend PI Planning in full
3. Be decisive on scope conflicts
4. Evaluate outcomes, not just outputs
5. Communicate strategic context clearly
6. Support ART in removing business blockers
